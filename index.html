FEATURED VARIETAL

Merlot

One of the “noble varietals” of the wine world, Merlot has often been thought of as the little brother of Cabernet Sauvignon, but opposed to Cabernet, Merlot’s greatest virtue is its relatively thinner grape skins, which give the wines a softer, suppler tannin structure.
